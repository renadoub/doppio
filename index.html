RELATIVE VALUE CURRENCY HEDGE FUND

The Doppio Systematic Currency Fund is a relative value currency hedge fund PROJECT.

The Fund run an active and adaptive strategy with the objective to generate a high and consistent long-term absolute risk-adjusted return.

The Fund mainly invests in ten of the most liquid currencies in the world: Australian dollar, British Pound, Canadian dollar, Euro, Japanese yen, Norwegian krona, New Zealand dollar, Swedish krona, Swiss Franc and United States dollar.

A proprietary model is used to forecast short term expected return on those currencies and the results are used in a portfolio optimizations model to maximize the Funds expected return subject to various risk limits. The resulting portfolio has no net exposure to the US Dollar. We believe this would ensure a high return/risk ratio.

Due to this nature of the Fund, the Fund has very low correlation to traditional asset classes such as Equities and Bonds over time.
